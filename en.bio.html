Bio

Andrew Arloro is an 18-year-old musician who lives on Long Island, New York. He is a recent graduate of Juilliard Pre-College and Half Hollow Hills High School East.

Andrew has studied the French Horn under Jen Montone, Principal Horn of the Philadelphia Orchestra; Jeff Lang, Associate Principal Horn of the Philadelphia Orchestra; Brad Gemeinhardt, a Principal Horn of the Metropolitan Opera; and other renowned musicians from top ensembles. Likewise, he currently studies with Erik Ralske, another Principal Horn of the Metropolitan Opera.

Last year, Andrew won the Discovery Competition for Children's Orchestra Society. He has performed solos in Carnegie Hall and Lincoln Center over a dozen times.

Andrew has had the honor of musically touring China, Spain, and Italy. He has been awarded the title of Principal Horn in various ensembles, including the Juilliard Orchestra, Juilliard Pre-College, All-State, SCMEA, Children's Orchestra Society, Gemini Youth Orchestras; and Cross Campus Wind Ensemble.

He currently studies with Erik Ralske at The Juilliard School, pursuing a Bachelor of Music in Performance on the French Horn.
