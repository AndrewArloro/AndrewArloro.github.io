My name is Andrew Arloro. I am a graduate of Juilliard Pre-College and a current undergrad at the Juilliard School and am curious about math and philosophy. Similarly, I enjoy many activities, such as playing music, rock climbing, skating, dancing, playing chess, exercising, playing board games, reading, listening to music, thinking, and learning! I grew up on Long Island with my parents, brothers, and cats, and emotional movement is significant to me as an artist. By creating art, I try to move people and inspire the youth. I want to show children that there are things of great worth within them.
